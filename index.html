FAB Balance Check Guide for UAE Users

Managing your finances starts with knowing your available bank balance at the right time. First Abu Dhabi Bank offers several official and secure ways to check account balances for personal and salary account holders in the UAE. Customers can access their balance using mobile banking, SMS, ATMs, WhatsApp, or assisted services depending on convenience. This guide explains each FAB Balance Check method with accurate steps and real usage details.

About First Abu Dhabi Bank

First Abu Dhabi Bank is the largest bank in the United Arab Emirates and was formed after the merger of First Gulf Bank and National Bank of Abu Dhabi. FAB serves individuals, salary earners, businesses, and corporate clients through a strong digital banking system and a wide branch network across the country.

The bank focuses on secure transactions, digital convenience, and customer accessibility.

Key highlights of FAB

  • Largest banking network in the UAE
  • Advanced mobile and online banking services
  • Secure debit card and ATM infrastructure
  • Support for salary, savings, and current accounts

Why Checking Your FAB Balance Regularly Matters

Regular balance checks help customers stay aware of available funds and avoid declined transactions. It also allows confirmation of salary credits, ATM withdrawals, and card payments. Monitoring balances frequently improves financial planning and prevents unexpected charges.

Regular balance checks help you

  • Confirm salary deposits on time
  • Avoid insufficient balance card declines
  • Track daily spending accurately
  • Detect unusual account activity early

Methods to Check FAB Account Balance

1. FAB Mobile App Balance Check

The FAB Mobile App is the most reliable and real-time way to check your account balance. It works for savings, current, and salary accounts.

Open the FAB Mobile App and log in using your registered username, password, or biometric authentication. Once logged in, your available balance and ledger balance are displayed on the home screen along with recent transactions.

  • App available on Android and iOS
  • Shows real-time balance
  • Supports multiple accounts under one login

2. FAB Official Website Online Banking

FAB also allows balance checks through its official online banking website. This method is suitable for users who prefer desktop access.

  • Best used for downloading statements
  • Helpful for managing multiple accounts
  • Ideal on laptop or desktop

3. FAB ATM Balance Check

Insert your FAB debit card into any FAB ATM, enter your PIN, and select the balance inquiry option. Your available balance appears on the screen, and you may print a receipt if required.

  • Works without internet
  • Available 24 hours
  • Free at FAB ATMs

4. FAB SMS Balance Inquiry

FAB provides a quick SMS balance check service for registered mobile numbers. Send BAL to 2121 from your registered UAE mobile number to receive your balance by SMS.

  • Mobile number must be registered with FAB
  • Works only within the UAE
  • SMS charges may apply

5. FAB WhatsApp Balance Check

FAB offers banking support through its official WhatsApp channel. Save +971 600 525500, start a chat, and follow the automated menu after verification.

6. FAB Customer Care Balance Inquiry

Dial 600 525500 inside the UAE and follow IVR instructions. After verification, the system or agent can provide balance details.

7. FAB Email Alerts and E-Statements

FAB allows customers to receive account updates through email alerts and periodic statements after enabling notifications in online banking or the app.

8. FAB Branch Balance Check

Visit any FAB branch with your Emirates ID and debit card. A representative can verify identity and provide your account balance.

9. FAB Balance Check via POS Terminals

Some merchants support balance inquiry via POS machines. Insert your card, enter PIN, and select balance inquiry if available.

Availability depends on the merchant POS configuration.

Common Problems While Checking FAB Balance

Some balance check methods may not work due to account or technical issues. These are usually temporary and easily resolved.

Common causes include

  • Incorrect login credentials
  • Unregistered mobile number for SMS
  • Blocked or expired debit card
  • Temporary system maintenance

Security Tips for FAB Balance Checks

  • Never share your PIN or OTP
  • Use only official FAB platforms
  • Avoid public WiFi for banking
  • Log out after online access

Frequently Asked Questions About FAB Balance Check

Are FAB balance checks available 24 hours

Yes. Mobile app, ATMs, SMS, and online banking are usually available 24 hours. Branch hours differ.

Do I need a registered mobile number for SMS balance check

Yes. SMS balance inquiry usually works only when your UAE number is registered with the bank.

Are balance inquiry methods free

Most methods are free. Standard SMS charges may apply depending on your mobile plan.

Conclusion

First Abu Dhabi Bank offers multiple secure options to check your account balance in the UAE. Customers can choose mobile apps, SMS, ATMs, WhatsApp, or branch services based on convenience. Regular checks support better planning and reduce payment issues when using official channels.
