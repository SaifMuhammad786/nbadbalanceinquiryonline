NBAD Balance Inquiry Online Guide

NBAD Balance Inquiry Online Guide explains how account holders in the UAE can check NBAD (now FAB) account balances using official and commonly used methods. This page provides clear steps and options for balance inquiry without having to visit a branch.

What Is NBAD Balance Inquiry

NBAD balance inquiry refers to checking the available funds in an NBAD account through digital services provided by the bank. Since NBAD merged with First Abu Dhabi Bank (FAB), most services are integrated into FAB’s systems.

Available Methods to Check Account Balance

There are multiple ways to check NBAD or FAB account balance. Account holders can use online banking, the official mobile application, ATMs, telephone banking, or visit a branch for assistance. Choose the method that best suits your convenience.

Online Banking

Users with online banking credentials can log in to the FAB internet banking portal to view their account balance and recent transactions. This is one of the fastest ways to check balance information securely.

Mobile Banking App

The FAB mobile app allows you to check your balance on the go. Download the official app from your device’s app store, sign in with your credentials, and select the account to see the balance instantly.

ATMs and Telephone Banking

You can also check your account balance at any FAB or NBAD associated ATM by inserting your debit card and entering your PIN. Telephone banking is another option where you can call the official number and follow prompts to hear your balance.

Important Tips for Secure Balance Inquiry

Always use official banking channels to check your balance and avoid entering sensitive information on third-party websites or apps. Keep your login credentials confidential and update passwords regularly for security.
